ABOUT US

At Mustang®, we are more than just a brand. We are a testament to the enduring spirit of family, shared values, and a commitment to evolution. For over 50 years, we have been an integral part of countless memories, dreams, and journeys around the world. Our story is one of adaptability and leadership, just like the iconic vehicle that bears our name.

Henry Ford transformed not only the automobile but working life and the role of transportation. Having played our part in everything from the birth of the middle class to the recent global pandemic, we serve the world with integrity and competence. The Blue Oval and Mustang® are two of the most recognized corporate symbols in history, continually striving to earn the trust of all stakeholders.

We are here for one purpose, to help build a better world, where every person is free to move and pursue their dreams. We build cars that are iconic and bold, with roaring engines that win hearts.

With the people of Mustang® around the world, our leadership is committed to serving all of our stakeholder groups. Like generations of leaders before them, they understand that by helping to create a world with fewer obstacles and limits, we help people to move forward and upward.
